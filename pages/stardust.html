STARDUST: A JOURNEY TO REMEMBER

A poetic and enchanting story that follows an adventurer named Naledi on an epic journey through inner and outer universes to remember the miracle it is to be alive. Written by Garrett Brill & Illustrated by Daniel Fleck!

With the support of an incredible community, the creators launched a Kickstarter campaign to bring this story to life through animation. After reaching their goal, they entrusted me with the task of translating the book's magic into motion! View the kickstarter here!
